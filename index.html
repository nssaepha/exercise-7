Who Is Naihla Dawkins?

Hii! Welcolme to my blog! To whom is reading, this is Naihla, pronounced NY-LA but most people call me Nai. Thats me! (on the far right) and those are my two best friends beside me with whom I share countless memories and adventures with.

I’m a full time student at the University of Waterloo studying Global Business and Digital Arts (i almost ran out of breath reading that too). I am in my second year of the program going into my 2B term this winter. Unlike a lot of people I cross paths with, I was born and raised in Waterloo and have lived here my whole life. When deciding on post secondary, I chose Waterloo because it’s close to home, the program is one of a kind, and I want to study abroad during my fourth term! I am passionate about design, business, and user experience and look forward to a career in this field post-graduation.

For any inquires regarding design projects, you can reach me through my school email here.

I come from a busy household of six. I have 3 sibilings, 1 older and 2 younger, all sisters (yes, I can feel the sympathy for my dad pouring in). My roots are a vibrant mix of Jamaican and Laotian heritage. It's always amusing to see people try to guess my ethnicity; the most common guesses include Cambodian, Filipina, or Thai. I once even got Latina lol!

When I’m not studying I spend my time working. I work part time at Footlocker and on top of that, I proudly manage my own business, NaiBeauti. Offering lash and nail services, I collaborate with women of all ages, helping them embrace and enhance their natural beauty.

My journey into the world of beauty started at the age of 16 when I got my eyelashes done for the first time ever and fell in love. I was obsessed with the way it made me feel. It was a transformative experience that left me feeling confident and beautiful. Since then, I have been getting them done since and was granted the oppurtunity to experince being on the other side of the service.

Being a business owner is more than a title to me; it's a journey filled with goals achieved, milestones reached, and doors opened. For those curious or looking to seek more information, feel free to contact me through any of the channels below or press me to visit my page.
